Rules for the Game of Amazons

  • The Game of Amazons is a turn-based board game, played by two players.
  • Every turn consists of two phases.
  • First phase involves moving any amazon(queen) of your color. They move like normal chess queens and can't jump over or capture anything.
  • In second phase the amazon you just moved needs to shoot an arrow, which also moves like a chess queen. The square it lands on, burns out and becomes inaccesible. No amazons or arrows will be able to land or go over this square.
  • The person who can't move any of their amazons loses and their opponent wins.
